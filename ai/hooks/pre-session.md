# Hook: Pre-Session Orientation

**Trigger:** Start of every new interaction or session.

## Purpose
Load project state and historical context to ensure continuity and prevent repetitive mistakes.

## Actions
1. **Context Load**: Identify and read the project's memory, session history, or design documents to understand recent decisions and status.
2. **Roadmap Sync**: Identify the project's active roadmap or task list to determine the current goals and remaining work.
3. **Environment Audit**: Perform a silent check of the project's toolchain and environment to ensure they are ready for work.

## Mandate
Do not propose new work until you have summarized the "Current State" based on the project's established documentation and history.
